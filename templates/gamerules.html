CHARACTER CREATION RULES

CHOOSE A RACE:

    Select a race (e.g., Human, Elf, Dwarf). Each race provides unique racial traits such as bonuses to ability scores, special skills, or abilities (e.g., darkvision, resistance to certain damage types).

CHOOSE A CLASS:

    Pick a class (e.g., Fighter, Wizard, Rogue) that defines your character’s role in combat and adventuring. Each class comes with unique abilities, hit points, and proficiencies. Your choice of class will influence your character’s skills and combat style.

DETERMINE ABILITY SCORES:

    Your character’s ability scores (Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma) determine your character's strengths and weaknesses. These scores will be determined either through rolling dice or using a point-buy system, based on the rules provided for character creation.

SELECT A BACKGROUND:

    Choose a background (e.g., Acolyte, Criminal, Noble) which provides additional proficiencies, languages, and personality traits to help define your character’s history and motivations.
