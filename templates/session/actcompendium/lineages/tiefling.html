TIEFLINGS

Player's Handbook

To be greeted with stares and whispers, to suffer violence and insult on the street, to see mistrust and fear in every eye: this is the lot of the tiefling. Their appearance and their nature are not their fault but the result of an ancient sin, for which they and their children will always be held accountable.

Main Traits

  • Ability Score Increase: Your Charisma score increases by 2.
  • Age: Tieflings mature at the same rate as humans but live a few years longer.
  • Alignment: Tieflings might not have an innate tendency toward evil, but many end up there, often leaning toward chaotic alignments.
  • Size: Tieflings are about the same size and build as humans. Your size is Medium.
  • Speed: Your base walking speed is 30 feet.
  • Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.
  • Hellish Resistance: You have resistance to fire damage.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Infernal.

Bloodline of Asmodeus

  • Ability Score Increase: Your Intelligence score increases by 1.
  • Infernal Legacy: You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip. At 3rd level, you can cast Hellish Rebuke once as a 2nd-level spell. At 5th level, you can also cast Darkness once.

Variant Tiefling

Some tieflings have traits that differ from those in the Player's Handbook. The Dungeon Master may permit the following variants for your tiefling character:

  • Appearance: Choose 1d4 + 1 features such as small horns, fangs, a forked tongue, or leathery skin.
  • Feral: Your Intelligence score increases by 1, and your Dexterity score increases by 2.
  • Devil's Tongue: You know the Vicious Mockery cantrip. At 3rd level, you can cast Charm Person once as a 2nd-level spell. At 5th level, you can also cast Enthrall once.
  • Hellfire: At 3rd level, you can cast Burning Hands once as a 2nd-level spell.
  • Winged: You have bat-like wings and a flying speed of 30 feet while not wearing heavy armor.

Abyssal Tiefling

All abyssal tieflings trace their bloodline to the demons of the Abyss. They have the following additional features:

  • Ability Score Increase: Your Constitution score increases by 1.
  • Abyssal Arcana: You gain the ability to cast cantrips and spells randomly determined from a short list.
  • Abyssal Fortitude: Your hit point maximum increases by half your level (minimum 1).
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Abyssal.
